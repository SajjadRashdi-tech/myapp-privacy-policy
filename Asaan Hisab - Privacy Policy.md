Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 12, 2026

Asaan Hisab ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Asaan Hisab collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our mobile app and web app.

1. Information We Collect

Asaan Hisab may collect the following information:

- Email address for login and OTP verification
- Business records you create in the app, including:
  - customer contacts
  - ledger entries
  - stock and inventory records
  - bills and purchase records
  - sales and payment records
- Device and app usage data required for app functionality
- Offline data stored temporarily on your device for sync when internet becomes available

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- authenticate your account through email OTP
- provide access to your account and records
- save and sync your business data
- show your own ledger, stock, bill, and sale information
- improve app reliability, security, and performance

3. Data Visibility

Your data is intended to be visible only to your own account. Each user can access only their own records stored for that account.

4. Offline Storage and Sync

Asaan Hisab may store data locally on your device when you are offline. Once internet access is available, the app may sync your pending data to our server so your records remain updated.

5. Permissions

Depending on the features you use, Asaan Hisab may request access to:

- Internet connection for login, sync, and server communication
- Camera for barcode scanning or related app features
- Storage or media access where required by your device for app-related functions

These permissions are used only for app functionality.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as needed to provide the app’s services, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

8. Third-Party Services

Asaan Hisab may use third-party infrastructure or service providers for hosting, email delivery, authentication support, or technical operations. These services may process data only as needed to provide app functionality.

9. Children’s Privacy

Asaan Hisab is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to:

- access your personal data
- request correction of inaccurate data
- request deletion of your data
- contact us regarding privacy concerns

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted with a revised effective date.
