Privacy Policy for TumbleTuck

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

TumbleTuck ("TumbleTuck," "we," "us," or "our") provides a game platform that lets users access games, create profiles, earn in-app rewards, and participate in multiplayer matches. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect information when you use the TumbleTuck mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using TumbleTuck, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

a. Information You Provide Directly

- Email address used to request and verify one-time passcodes (OTP) for login
- Verification code entries submitted during authentication
- Profile information such as display name, selected avatar, and gender
- Information you provide when joining matches, sending requests through the app, or contacting support

b. Account and Session Information

- Internal user ID
- Session token and session-related identifiers
- Authentication status and login records

c. Gameplay and Activity Information

- Games viewed or launched
- Match IDs, room IDs, game settings, and multiplayer room participation data
- Gameplay outcomes, rankings, rewards, coin balances, streaks, and bonus claims
- In-app notifications related to matches, rewards, and activity

d. Device, Network, and Technical Information

- Basic device/platform information used by the app to operate correctly
- App diagnostics such as connection failures, request errors, and crash-related technical information if generated by the platform or service providers
- IP address and network-related information that may be processed by our servers or service providers when you connect to the Service

e. Information Stored on Your Device

- Login state and authentication data stored locally
- Cached API responses and session cookies stored using app storage mechanisms
- Temporary game or app state needed to keep the Service working

f. Advertising and Analytics-Related Information

- Advertising identifiers or similar device identifiers where supported by your device and applicable law
- Ad interaction data that may be collected by ad partners when ads are shown

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information:

- Directly from you when you log in, edit your profile, start or join matches, claim rewards, or use sharing features
- Automatically when you use the Service
- From your device and app storage when restoring your session or loading saved preferences
- From service providers and infrastructure that support authentication, hosting, advertising, notifications, and multiplayer connectivity

3. How We Use Your Information

We use information for purposes including:

- Creating and maintaining your account
- Sending and verifying OTP login codes
- Authenticating users and preventing unauthorized access
- Providing gameplay, matchmaking, multiplayer rooms, and invite links
- Saving profile settings and displaying your in-app identity to you and other players
- Calculating rewards, daily bonus streaks, rank, and game outcomes
- Operating app features such as sharing room links, copying invite codes, and loading game content
- Serving ads and measuring ad delivery where ads are enabled
- Maintaining security, debugging issues, and improving app stability and performance
- Enforcing our terms, preventing abuse, fraud, cheating, or misuse of the Service
- Complying with legal obligations

4. Legal Bases for Processing

Where required by applicable law, we process personal information on one or more of the following bases:

- Performance of a contract, such as providing the Service you request
- Legitimate interests, such as securing the Service, preventing abuse, and improving gameplay
- Consent, where required for certain permissions, notifications, or advertising-related processing
- Compliance with legal obligations

Depending on your device, platform, and the features you use, TumbleTuck may request or use permissions such as:

- Internet and network access, to connect to our servers and multiplayer services
- Camera access, if a camera-enabled feature is used

- Advertising ID access, where available for ad-related functions

We do not claim ownership of content on your device. Permissions are used only to support app features and platform requirements.

When you start or join multiplayer matches, some information is shared with other participants or used to run the match, including:

- Your in-app profile identity, such as display name, avatar, or user ID

If you use invite, share, or copy features, invite links or room details may be generated and shared through third-party apps or services that you choose. Once shared outside TumbleTuck, the handling of that information is governed by the privacy practices of those third-party services.
